Odds and Rules

Game Rules

Clean reference for pack odds, trade rules, confidence and league modes.

Standard Pack

70% Common · 22% Silver · 7% Gold · 1% Diamond

Guarantees 3 Common and 1 Silver+.

Rare Pack

45% Common · 38% Silver · 14% Gold · 3% Diamond

Guarantees 2 Common and 2 Silver+.

Confidence

All players start at 50. Lowest 1, highest 200. Wins increase confidence exponentially. Losses reduce it. Confidence boosts or nerfs flair behaviour.

Guard Rails

Starter pack items are untradeable. Loaned, injured and suspended cards cannot be used in games or listed.
